About Me

My name is Matthew Allen. I was born and raised in south Alabama on the Eastern Shore of Mobile Bay. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Auburn University and a Master of Arts in Psychology from The University of West Florida. I spent the first 5 years of my professional career as an intensive in-home therapist for at risk youth.

I have worked as an in-home therapist in Florida, Alabama, & North Carolina. I learned a lot about people, families, and what motivates individuals to do various things throughout life. It was a truly enjoyable and fullfilling job, but ultimately I burned out. Once I started to feel compassion fatigue and realized I was not able to continue therapy at my best, I left the field for something new.

From there, the decision was quite simple for me. I have always loved technology, from taking a part my xbox as a teenager just to see how it worked, to reading about userscripts online and how they can automate actions for you. As an adult, I have found a hobby in building and selling CryptoCurrency mining rigs. I taught myself how to build them from the ground up as well as program the machine to do exactly what I needed. All of this made it clear to me that I wanted to be in the world of technology and I wanted to learn as much as I could.

When the opportunity to attend The Coding Bootcamp at UNC was presented to me, I jumped in head first, and it has been the greatest decision of my career. I have learned many different development tools, both front-end and back-end. WHile I enjoy both sides, I have truly found my passion to be front-end development. I love working up a design, pseudocoding it out, and then making it come to fruition and look exactly how I want. I have grown to love HTML, CSS, & Javascript and I often continue to expand my knowledge of these tools in my spare time. I know I have what it takes to be an amazing front-end developer and I look forward to working with a company that can utilize my talents to the fullest extent.
